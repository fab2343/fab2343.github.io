¿Who is Movy?

Movy is a skilled gamer and content creator known for their prowess in multiplayer games and their active presence on platforms like YouTube, Twitch, and TikTok..

Movy's Journey

Movy's journey in the gaming world began back in 2007 when they started playing as a sniper on Counter-Strike: Source, using a keyboard and mouse at the age of 7. At 12, they transitioned to playing Call of Duty on a controller, whether it was Xbox or PlayStation. Throughout the years, they mastered various multiplayer games using controllers until Modern Warfare 2019, where they made the switch to keyboard and mouse. Despite starting with keyboard and mouse at a young age, it wasn't until later that they fully embraced it.

Movy's YouTube channel was created on July 17, 2012. However, they gained prominence primarily through TikTok, where their first video was uploaded on August 12, 2022. Their oldest clip on YouTube dates back 11 years, showcasing their journey and evolution over time.
